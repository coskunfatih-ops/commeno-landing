What data does Commeno access?

  • Your YouTube channel ID and public profile (name, profile picture) — to identify your account.
  • Your YouTube videos, comments, and replies — to display them in the Commeno workspace.
  • Your live stream chat messages — to show them alongside regular comments.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens — stored encrypted on our servers, used only to communicate with the YouTube API on your behalf.
  • Reply drafts and sent reply records — stored in our database so you can track what you've responded to.
  • Your "Save for Later" marks and sync history — to power workspace features across sessions.
